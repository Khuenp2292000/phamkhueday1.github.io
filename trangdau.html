“We continue” – Cristiano Ronaldo update after solo training session

Pham Khue, October 20, 2022

Cristiano Ronaldo took to Instagram last night to post an update following a solo training session at Carrington on Friday.In the photo – shared to Instagram stories – Ronaldo is shown wearing inflatable recovery sleeves on his legs, and he wrote the caption: “we continue”. Ronaldo is trying to get past what happened on Wednesday night when he refused to come off the bench and left the stadium prematurely. The cameras caught the 37-year-old storming towards the tunnel on the 89th minute and it wasn’t a good look for the ‘role model’.As a result, Ronaldo has been axed from the squad to face Chelsea.

De Gea full of praise for Omonia goalkeeper after amazing performance

Pham Khue, October 15, 2022

Manchester United goalkeeper David de Gea was full of praise for Omonia shot-stopper Francis Uzoho, even though the visitors lost the game on the night. I loved Uzoho’s post-match interview on BT Sport because he was so proud of his performance as well as the fact the Nigeria international is a massive United fan certainly added to it. That said, I don’t think I would have enjoyed it so much if it wasn’t for Scott McTominay’s late winner.

Antony names one Man United player who has helped him since signing from Ajax

Pham Khue, October 12, 2022

Antony has hit the ground running since signing for Manchester United from Ajax on deadline day, with the Brazilian star becoming the first United player to score in his first three Premier League appearances.The signing was almost immediately questioned by pundits and experts but the 22-year-old has already proved some people wrong. Paul Scholes, who was critical of the winger, has already reacted to being called out.Antony has instantly become a first-team regular under Erik ten Hag, with whom he worked for two-and-a-half years at Ajax, starting all league games thus far.
